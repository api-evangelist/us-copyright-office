# US Copyright Office (us-copyright-office)
The US Copyright Office is a government agency responsible for administering and enforcing copyright laws in the United States. The office is responsible for registering and documenting copyright claims, as well as providing information and resources to help creators understand and protect their intellectual property rights. Additionally, the office plays a key role in resolving copyright disputes and ensuring compliance with copyright laws. The US Copyright Office works to promote creativity and innovation by safeguarding the rights of creators and ensuring that their work is protected from infringement.
